TIPS AND TRICKS

  • The green circle Γ is Γαῖα, the Earth, and the yellow circles Σ and M moving about it are Σήλιος and Mήλιος, the Suns.
  • The red circles Α and Β represent points around which Σ and M uniformly orbit.
  • The light blue circles represent the uniform orbits of bodies.
  • The red lines represent the uniform positions of bodies along their orbits.
  • The yellow lines represent the apparent positions of bodies to the Earth Γ.
  • Fiddling with the buttons and knobs can result in interesting extensions of Ptolemy's system.
